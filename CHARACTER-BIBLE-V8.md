# LEGACY Pro Wrestling — Character Bible 8.0

The finished `full.webp` artwork is the canonical source for physical appearance. Early brainstorming descriptions are superseded whenever they conflict with the artwork.

## Founding Roster

### “Iceman” Jack Mercer
Bald, heavily muscled brawler with a black goatee. Black sleeveless entrance vest, black trunks and pads, black boots with red detailing, taped wrists and a defiant two-handed gesture. Tattoos cover both arms and extend across the upper chest.

### Victor Royale
Tall, broad-shouldered powerhouse with long straight black hair and a clean-shaven face. Purple-and-gold trunks and matching structured shoulder gear, black knee pads and boots, gold wrist tape. His appearance is regal, controlled and severe.

### Jett Valentine
Lean, athletic showman with long wavy brown hair, pink wraparound sunglasses and a confident smile. Bright pink sleeveless vest, white-and-pink tights with heart graphics, white boots and pink accessories.

### The Revenant
Large, pale supernatural figure with long grey hair, a gaunt face and dark eye detailing. Long black-and-purple robe-like entrance gear over black clothing and heavy black boots. His hands are extended in a cold, commanding pose.

### Nightwatch
Tall, lean dark enforcer with long black hair and stark pale facial colouring. Long black coat, dark purple shirt and tie, black trousers and boots. The finished design is formal, shadowy and restrained.

### “Hollywood” Titan
Muscular, dark-haired celebrity figure wearing black sunglasses. Open burnt-orange dress shirt, black trousers and black shoes. The finished artwork does **not** use the original long blond hair and red wrestling outfit concept; this modern Hollywood appearance is canonical.

### Mason Marks
Lean technical wrestler with long straight black hair and a clean-shaven face. Red-and-white Canadian singlet with maple-leaf graphics, white boots and red detailing. Hands rest confidently at his hips.

### Hollowman
Huge masked horror figure wearing a weathered black hood and mask. Blood-stained butcher’s apron over dark clothing, heavy black gloves and boots, with scarred or marked exposed arms. His design is grounded slasher horror rather than conventional wrestling glamour.

### Damian Black
Compact, athletic striker with short black hair and a clean-shaven face. Dark green trunks, matching knee pads and tall green lace-up boots with pale trim. Minimal accessories and a calm, precise stance.

### Elias Crowe
Stocky, wild-eyed brawler with messy dark hair, beard stubble and an exaggerated grin. Black clothing under a torn dark-green sleeveless overshirt, black trousers and heavy boots. His visual identity is dishevelled, unstable and dangerous.

### El Rey del Cielo
Athletic masked luchador in a metallic blue-and-white mask. Open white sleeveless hooded vest with blue trim, blue-and-white tights, white boots and matching wrist tape. Clean celestial colour palette and heroic posture.

### Max Justice
Muscular blond hero with short hair and a clean-shaven face. Blue tights with large white star graphics, blue-and-white boots and matching blue wrist and arm bands. Bright, traditional superhero presentation.

### Primal
Massive, heavily muscled wildman with a bald crown, thick dark beard and fierce expression. Fur-trimmed brown vest, green-and-brown animal-pattern trunks, fur wrist guards and fur-trimmed boots.

### Lucas Bennett
Athletic collegiate wrestler with short brown hair and a clean-shaven face. Red, white and blue singlet marked “USA” and “BENNETT,” white boots with blue detailing and white wrist tape. Confident technical-wrestling hand pose.

### Marcus King
Powerful Black wrestler with shoulder-length dreadlocks and a short beard. Deep red sleeveless top and matching trousers, black wrist guards and heavy black boots, with gold chains around his neck.

### Mateo Vega
Lean, charismatic wrestler with styled black hair, moustache and pointed beard. Elaborate teal, white, black and gold sleeveless long-coat gear with matching tights and white boots. Finger-gun pose reinforces his con-artist confidence.

### Ryder Phoenix
Lean rockstar with long bright-red hair and a short beard. Black-and-red sleeveless vest, black tights with red flame-like graphics, black gloves and boots. His finished design is darker and more hard-rock than colourful pop-star.

### Sterling Sinclair
Tall, muscular blond with shoulder-length hair and a clean-shaven face. Purple trunks and boots beneath an extravagant sparkling emerald-green entrance robe with pale fur trim. One hand rests thoughtfully at his chin.

### Dave Maddox
Large, rugged veteran with long curly light-brown hair and a full beard. Dark-blue sleeveless vest and matching trousers, black knee pads and boots. Straightforward workhorse presentation.

### Logan Steele
Older, deeply tanned powerhouse with long grey hair and a large grey handlebar moustache. Red trunks, red knee pads and red boots with orange wrist tape. Classic flexing hero pose.

## Version 8 Expansion

### Ace Riot — “The Voice of Rebellion”
Lean, tattooed technical wrestler with medium dark hair, short beard and intense expression. Black trunks with restrained red detailing, black pads and boots, red-laced boots, white wrist tape and black forearm gear. Arms folded across his chest; tattoos cover both arms and upper chest.

### Everest — “The Immovable Mountain”
Enormous natural giant with short black hair, a broad face and a calm expression. One-strap forest-green singlet with gold trim, black-and-gold knee pads and boots, black wrist tape. Massive proportions without exaggerated bodybuilder definition.

### Axel Voss — “The Human Wrecking Ball”
Extremely muscular combat athlete with close-cropped blond hair and a stern clean-shaven face. Black fight shorts with subtle dark-red trim, black gloves, knee pads and boots. Compact stance and minimal ornamentation emphasise athletic violence.

### Slater Nova — “The Freefall Artist”
Lean high-flyer with long dark hair, vivid purple-and-cyan face paint and colourful sleeves. Black cargo-style trousers with purple and cyan accents, black boots and fingerless gloves. His artwork is abstract, modern and expressive rather than gothic.

## Canonical Version 8 Archetypes

- Ace Riot — The Rebel
- Everest — The Attraction
- Axel Voss — The Destroyer
- Slater Nova — The Artist

These archetypes guide match behaviour, commentary, interviews, social reaction, ranking movement and championship booking.
