# Tag Team Gauntlet 6.5.1 — Living Television Test Notes

## Major changes
- Monday Night Mayhem and Thursday Night Throwdown branded television shows.
- Four-screen new-career introduction hosted by Veronica Vale.
- Starting choices: Jack Mercer, Victor Royale, Jett Valentine and The Revenant.
- Dynamic monthly booking plan: singles capped at four, tag matches capped at two, one multi-person match maximum, and replacement story segments.
- Rival is not automatically booked in every television match.
- Named rotating Supercards.
- Supercard result alone decides the feud. A successful feud unlocks the rival.
- End-of-month active wrestler selection.
- Dynamic non-show days that react to injuries and story priorities.
- Katie Morgan rename and expanded contextual interviews.
- World results and commentary recaps from Mike Sullivan and Johnny Cannon.
- New NPC artwork integrated across story screens.
- PNG artwork converted to WebP.

## Recommended test
1. Start a new career and complete all four Veronica Vale introduction screens.
2. Confirm the four flagship founder choices.
3. Play one full month and record the eight Mayhem/Throwdown slots. No more than four should be singles matches.
4. Confirm opponents vary and the feud rival is not used for every match.
5. Trigger an injury and confirm Dr. Lena Hart replaces the next normal activity.
6. Complete the named Supercard. Confirm its winner decides the feud and a victory recruits the rival.
7. Select a wrestler for the next month.
