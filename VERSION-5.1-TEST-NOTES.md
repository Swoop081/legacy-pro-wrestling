# Tag Team Gauntlet 5.1 — Mobile Presentation & Flow

This release consolidates the mobile presentation fixes reported during Version 5.0 Preview 2 testing.

## Main changes
- Paused all player-facing faction references.
- Standardized new wrestler artwork scaling across Home, Quick Match, Collections and Classic Gauntlet.
- Corrected Collection profile image containment.
- Compact Singles and Tag Team exhibition previews.
- Removed redundant playable labels and team-name duplication.
- Raised live match moment/time status.
- Reduced live legal-wrestler card headroom.
- Centered the YOUR CALL tab.
- Corrected win/loss result wording and enlarged victory artwork.
- Uses team-safe celebration text in Tag Team results.
- Places result headlines below the winners.
- Compact one-screen reward selection.
- Compact two-choice partner selection.
- Automatic transitions after partner confirmation and reward application.
- Streamlined challenger presentation: wrestlers, VS, commentary desk and venue only.
- Removed repetitive entrance narration.
- Corrected Second Wind messaging to describe the player's team.

## Testing priorities
- iPhone-sized viewport: confirm key decision and preview screens do not require scrolling.
- Confirm all wrestler art remains contained without head or foot clipping.
- Test Singles and Tag Team Quick Match win and loss result text.
- Trigger Second Wind after a loss and verify the player's team is named.
- Test rewards that retain the team and rewards that replace a wrestler.
