# LEGACY Pro Wrestling 8.4.2 — Living World Completion

## Main additions
- Living World career dashboard with current wrestler, World Champion, rival, recent form, next show and current headlines.
- Persistent News Network covering rankings, champion watch, show previews, form, tournament results and month-end stories.
- Expanded wrestler-owned career history: ranking highs/lows, records, streaks, SuperCards, tournament wins and World Championship reigns.
- Roster screen now presents each recruited wrestler as a living career and links to their history.
- Returning to a wrestler after time under CPU control produces a factual “Previously On” summary.
- Full March #1 Contender Tournament bracket view and persistent tournament history hooks.
- Championship lineage, reign and defence tracking for the single LPW World Championship.
- Television framing additions: Tonight on LPW, Coming Up Next, world-aware show previews and dashboard headlines.
- Power Rankings remain the unified standings.
- Character switching remains restricted to after the monthly SuperCard.
- CPU-controlled wrestlers can change records, streaks, status and rankings, but cannot gain XP, levels, stat points or attributes.
