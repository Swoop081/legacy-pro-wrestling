# LEGACY Pro Wrestling v8.2.5 Build 1

## Outcome Continue Hotfix

- Fixes the game-breaking Match Psychology outcome overlay that could remain stuck on “Updating the live match…”.
- The CONTINUE button is now rendered directly in the active Career decision outcome template.
- The match remains paused until CONTINUE is pressed.
- Pressing CONTINUE clears the outcome, returns to the live match hub and resumes the match.

4. Press CONTINUE and confirm the broadcast resumes.
