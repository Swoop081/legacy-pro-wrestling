# Tag Team Gauntlet 5.0 Preview 1

## New systems
- Permanent manager recruitment during a Classic Gauntlet run.
- Four test managers with persistent passive match bonuses.
- Expanded weighted random-event engine replacing the Singles Challenge-only flow.
- Backstage interviews with Mean Gena and three response choices.
- Interview answers can improve chemistry, momentum, or next-match score.
- Training, media, scouting, manager recruitment, interview, and Singles Challenge events.
- Active manager display on the team screen and live match broadcast.
- Elias Crowe full, portrait, and victory artwork registered in Image Manager.

## Test path
1. Start Classic Gauntlet and win the opening match.
2. Take the guaranteed first reward.
3. Win additional matches and continue from the result screen.
4. Random events now have a 72% chance to trigger between later matches.
5. Recruit a manager and confirm they remain active across matches.
6. Trigger a Mean Gena interview and confirm the selected bonus appears on the team screen and match score.

Championships and the Road Manager hub are deliberately reserved for the future Gauntlet Live update.
