# LEGACY Pro Wrestling 8.4.3 — Battle Royal Playable Test

## New playable mode
A standalone **20-Person Battle Royal** is now available from the main menu.

## Included systems
- Choose one wrestler; nineteen unique opponents are selected randomly.
- Dedicated Battle Royal engine separate from Singles and Tag Team logic.
- Four-across portrait grid with five initial rows.
- Individual stamina/fatigue bars and vulnerability states.
- CPU-versus-CPU attacks, fatigue changes and eliminations.
- Battle Royal-only decisions including stamina conservation, hiding, attacking, crowd rallying, finishers, temporary alliances, brawls, double eliminations and context-sensitive elimination attempts.
- Outcome resolution with major success, success, failure and major failure states.
- Full-screen elimination flash and automatic grid reflow.
- Opening, middle, closing and Final Two pacing phases.
- Dedicated Final Two presentation and choices.
- Continue watching or simulate to the winner after player elimination.
- Winner, finishing position, most eliminations, longest survival and complete elimination order.
- Standalone Battle Royal history stored locally for testing.

## Career status
The match is not yet inserted into Career Mode. Once verified, it is intended to become the September SuperCard with the winner earning an October World Championship match.

## Testing priorities
1. Confirm all twenty portraits fit and remain readable on iPhone.
2. Check CPU eliminations do not happen too quickly or too slowly.
3. Verify the player can naturally win and lose.
4. Verify eliminated portraits disappear and the grid reflows correctly.
5. Test all decision types and Final Two logic.
6. Check spectator mode and simulate-to-winner.
7. Review results, elimination order and history persistence.
