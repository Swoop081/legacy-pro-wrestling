# LEGACY v8.2.3 Build 1 — Match Psychology 2.2

## Included
- Decision cards now show only the custom decision name; explanatory hint text is removed.
- Decision names are larger and the cards are cleaner and easier to scan.
- Expanded the decision-name pool across opening, control, crisis and finish phases.
- Career matches no longer receive the hidden first-match success floor.
- Outcome tuning now targets a much broader spread of Major Success, Success, Mixed Result, Failure and Major Failure.
- Wrestler attributes still influence outcomes, but no choice is guaranteed.
- Mixed Results now create genuine trade-offs: one or two meters improve while another falls.
- Failure and Major Failure remain capable of handing momentum and score to the opponent.

## Test focus
1. Play several Career matches with different wrestlers.
2. Confirm decision cards contain names only.
3. Confirm Mixed, Failure and Major Failure occur naturally.
4. Confirm Mixed Results can reduce Score, Control or Crowd while improving another value.
5. Confirm 3–5 decisions still appear per match.
